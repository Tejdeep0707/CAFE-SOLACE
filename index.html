Our Journey

The Art of Solace

Founded in 2026, Café Solace was born out of a simple dream: to create a cozy, premium refuge away from the hectic rush of everyday life. Our name reflects our commitment to being your sanctuary, offering a warm and inviting atmosphere where you can pause, breathe, and enjoy life's small pleasures.

We source only the finest single-origin beans, ethically harvested from small-scale farms in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Sumatra. Each batch is micro-roasted in-house weekly to bring out its distinctive character. Complementing our craft coffee is a curated menu of food and pastries, baked fresh daily by local culinary artists.
